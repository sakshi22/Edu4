JEE Main 2021 News: JEE Main Exam To Be Conducted Four Times In 2021, First Edition From February 23 to 26
16 Dec 2020
The Joint Entrance Examination or the JEE Mains Exam is to be conducted four times in 2021 starting from February to May, according to the Union Minister for Education Dr. Ramesh Pokhriyal Nishank with the objective of providing greater flexibility to students.
Supreme Court Directs States To Give Rs. 2000 A Month For Education Of Children Who Were In CCIs Amid Pandemic
Dec 16 2020
The Supreme Court of India while hearing a 'suo motu' case on the plight of children throughout the country in the juvenile, protection and foster homes during the COVID-19 pandemic has directed all states to give Rs. 2,000 per month for the education of children who were at the child care institutions (CCIs) and now restored to their families.
CDS Results 2020: UPSC Releases CDS Written Exam (II) Result 2020 On upsc.co.in
16 Dec 2020
The Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) has declared the Combined Defence Services (CDS) (II) 2020 result. Candidates who have appeared for the written exam held on November 8 can check their CDS results 2020 on the official website - www.upsc.gov.in. The sources suggest that a total of 6727 candidates have qualified in the CDS (II) exam 2020.
