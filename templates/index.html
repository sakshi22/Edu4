About us

Edu4 is a free-learning website providing courses of 4 streams on a single platform. It helps those people to learn just by signing in and providing free online courses. It also provides scholarship to the students who can excel in studies but can’t do because of lack of money.

Our objective is to:

• Enhance the quality of learning and teaching, by meeting the learning style/needs of the users.

• Provide scholarships to students who need financial support.

• Provide news related to education.

Achievements

  • More than 8,000 people got scholarships for further studies.
  • Helped more than 500 people to achieve their education and jobs.
  • 100 companies partnered with Edu4 to provide study materials for humanities,medical and more.
